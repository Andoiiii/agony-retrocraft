$\F^+$/$\F^+_0$ is the positive/non-negative subset of $\F$.
$\N = \Z^+ = \\{1,2,\dotsc\\}$.
$f(I)$ is the range of $f$ on $I$.
$C^n([a,b])$ is the set of functions with continuous $n$-th derivatives on $[a,b]$.
