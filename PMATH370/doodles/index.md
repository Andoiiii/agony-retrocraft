## Chapter 11: Julia set

#### Fact 11.2.15

<div class="derivations">
<p style="flex:1">
  Let $$z$$ be a point on the unit circle.
  Then, pick a small $$\varepsilon > 0$$
  and draw a neighbourhood $$z \in B_\varepsilon(z) \subseteq \C$$.
  Iterating, we cover the whole plane.
